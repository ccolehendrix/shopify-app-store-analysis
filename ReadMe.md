Analyst Memo-Shopify App Store Insights



Dashboard: Shopify App Store Analysis

Reporting Period: Latest available data



Key Insight:

The number of reviews have been rising year after year on our App and the average rating is 4.19 out of 5.



Business Impact:

The fact that the number of reviews on average is 4.19 out of 5 and that the number of reviews has risen each year, indicates that our users are getting new users to join and that the customers are on average fairly happy with the app, and most reviews happen in the summer, fall, and wither months.



Recommendation:

Any new changes to the app nee to happen during the spring, so that we can hopefully fix any new bugs that are happening in time for the busier seasons (summer, fall, and winter).
